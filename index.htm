01 / About

Who I Am

I'm an IT Automation Specialist studying Philosophy (BA) with an AI minor at LMU München — a combination that helps me think rigorously about both the technical and ethical dimensions of intelligent systems.

My path hasn't been straightforward. I grew up in Sevastopol, Crimea, finished school there in 2021, then moved to Kyiv to study Philosophy at university. In 2022, after the full-scale invasion of Ukraine, I rebuilt my life in Munich — learning German from scratch while working in parallel.

Professionally, I specialise in building process automation pipelines — from WhatsApp bots to real estate CRM systems — using Python, n8n, and Make. I'm self-taught in IT and thrive at the intersection of logic, systems thinking, and real-world impact.

My goal is to move into AI consulting, where I can apply both my technical depth and philosophical grounding to help organisations navigate the AI transition.
